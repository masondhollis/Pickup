Our Motivation.

Every day thousands of pick up sports games are played across the world. Everywhere you look there are communities built around these pickup games. But even within the same city there are distinct groups that are often unaware of one another.

Our dream at Pickup is to connect all these players in one place so they can see where they stack up, globally or in their area, and maybe find some new teammates while doing it.
